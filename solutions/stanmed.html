athenaOne® Healthcare Network Solutions

Ease clinical complexity while improving your financial performance

Let 's connect

Our suite of EHR, RCM, and patient engagement solutions and services enable outstanding care while maximizing revenue .

An EHR that lets patients be the focus

Physicians often feel like they have two jobs – one clinical and one administrative. athenaOne’s EHR makes it easier to focus on patients with specialty-specific workflows, automatic patient record sharing, real-time patient insights, and a mobile app that helps reduce documentation time.

RCM services that help increase revenue and decrease risk

Payer requirements are complex and changing, patient payments are hard to collect, and finding qualified billing staff is challenging. Our RCM services combine intelligent technology with expert support from real people to reduce administrative work and maximize your collections.

Patient engagement services that strengthen relationships

Your patients have higher expectations than ever when it comes to their healthcare experience. athenaOne empowers them with tools that make it easy to access health information, care services, and their care team while reducing administrative work for your staff.

A network that improves care delivery

Join a network that keeps your organization’s healthcare IT current with minimal disruption and exchanges data with pharmacies, labs, payers, and more

  • Total financial visibility

    Get an instant snapshot of your financial performance and actionable advice with our Insights Dashboards.

  • Interoperability

    Exchange integrated clinical data with external healthcare IT entities and other partners across the athenaOne network.

  • Real-time insights

    Make more informed operational and clinical decisions with insights from the largest network in healthcare.

Customer support focused on your success

Expert guidance from your Customer Success Manager and consistent support from day one means you’re never left wondering where to turn.

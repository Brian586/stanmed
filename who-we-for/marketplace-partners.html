Scale your solution with the largest, single, connected network in healthcare

Become a Marketplace partner

Connect with practices who need your solutions

The athenahealth Marketplace is our app store where customers can shop for integrated solutions. We have the most open, scalable platform in healthcare, making it easy for you to integrate your offering, promote it, and distribute it to our growing network of providers.

The benefits of a Marketplace partnership

Access to the most robust platform in healthcare

No need to worry about local installs, rebuilding, or scaling your solution. We now curate over 800+ API endpoints with documentation publicly available on our Developer Portal.* Effortlessly scale adoption without new customer development work on our single-instance, multi-tenant cloud platform.

Tools and resources that support growth

Joining the Marketplace gives you access to co-branded collateral, marketing templates, and targeted distribution to help promote your solution. You’ll also get a dynamic Marketplace page where you can demonstrate your solution, tell your value story, and receive automated leads.

Membership in the Marketplace community

Get the most out of your athenahealth partnership with the support of a dedicated Partner Success Manager. Connect with our community of over 240 like-minded healthcare technologists and thought leaders. Opportunities for involvement include networking events, newsletters, a community portal, and marketing campaigns.

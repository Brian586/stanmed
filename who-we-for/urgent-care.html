athenaOne® for Urgent Care

A comprehensive solution that maximizes efficiency and fuels growth

Let’s connect

athenaOne for Urgent Care combines tailored EHR, RCM, and patient engagement software with services that improve revenue, patient outreach, and efficiency.   

Transformative support to help you thrive

OUR URGENT CARE SOLUTION

Keep your care centers operating at peak efficiency with support that ensures patients can access your location, RCM services that help improve collections, and a solution that supports a single, fully integrated patient chart to ease the transition between care settings should you decide to expand your services. 

INSIDE OUR URGENT CARE SOLUTION

Grow your business through improved efficiency and experiences

Optimize clinical efficiency
​

Streamline every stage of the patient visit

From finding your location through payment, athenaOne helps you serve more patients faster and reduces staff burden without compromising care quality.
Urgent care-specific workflows

Intuitive, customizable workflows, driven by condition-specific encounter plans, help providers quickly and effectively treat patients without additional administrative burden. 

Accelerate documentation

Streamline charting and maximize patient throughput with condition-driven documentation accelerators, including macros, templates, encounter plans, and more. 

Reduce data exchange friction

Enable better-informed care with more complete clinical data through athenaOne’s built-in connectivity. It connects you to labs, imaging centers, health information exchanges, and a continually growing list of clinical entities in the broader healthcare ecosystem. 

E &M coding support

Stay compliant and avoid defensive downcoding with support from our E &M coder.
